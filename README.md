My Legacy — Islamic Estate Planner
Plan with purpose. Leave with peace.
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ
Death is a certainty. What we leave behind — and how we leave it — is a trust (amanah) we owe to Allah, to our families, and to those who depend on us. Yet for most Muslim families, estate planning remains unfinished business: intentions are good, but the paperwork never gets done.
My Legacy changes that. It's a private, secure, offline-first Islamic estate planning app that guides you through creating a Shariah-compliant Will, calculating the correct Faraid inheritance shares for your heirs, and keeping everything organised in one place — for yourself and your whole family.
No subscriptions. No cloud accounts. No lawyers needed to get started. Just open the app, enter your password, and begin.

Features
🔐 Private & Encrypted by Design
Your data never leaves your device. Everything is protected with AES-256 encryption — the same standard used by banks — secured by a password only you know. Even if someone had access to your phone or computer, your estate data would be unreadable without your password.
👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Multi-Profile Family Management
Create separate profiles for every family member — spouse, parents, children — each with their own independent Will and estate calculation. One app covers the whole family.
📜 Islamic Will Creator
Step-by-step guided Will creation covering everything that matters:

Personal details and declaration of faith (Shahada)
Funeral and burial wishes (including Ghusl, Kafan, Janazah, and cemetery preferences)
Outstanding Islamic obligations — Hajj, Zakah, Qard, Kaffarah
Appointment of Executors, Guardians, and Islamic Arbiters
Wasiyyah (charitable bequest, capped at one-third as per Shariah)
Waqf (charitable endowment)
Digital signature capture for testator and two witnesses
Full printable Will document, formatted for England & Wales

⚖️ Faraid Inheritance Calculator
A rigorous Islamic inheritance calculator built on classical Sunni fiqh:

Supports all four madhabs — Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, and Hanbali
Correctly applies Quranic fixed shares (fara'id), residuary (asabah), blocking (hajb), and return (radd)
Covers the full range of heirs: spouse, children, parents, grandparents, and siblings
Visual pie and bar charts showing each heir's share at a glance
Printable Faraid report for sharing with family or your solicitor

💾 Secure Backup & Restore
Export your encrypted data as a single backup file and import it on any other device. Transfer between your phone, tablet, and desktop without any cloud service. Your backup is just as encrypted as the original — safe to store or share.
🔑 Password Management
Change your password at any time from Settings. If you ever need to start over, a secure reset option is available — with clear warnings that encrypted data cannot be recovered without the original password.
🌙 Light & Dark Mode
A clean, calm interface available in light and dark themes — designed to feel thoughtful and trustworthy, not clinical.
📱 Works Everywhere
Runs entirely in your browser — no installation required. Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop. Can be saved to your home screen for a native app experience. Works fully offline once loaded.

Who Is This For?
My Legacy is for any Muslim who wants to fulfil their Islamic and legal obligations around estate planning — whether you're just getting started or updating an existing Will. It's especially valuable for:

Heads of household planning for their family's future
Parents wanting to protect their children's guardianship
Anyone with assets, property, or savings to pass on
Families who want Shariah-compliant Faraid distribution handled correctly

My Legacy is a planning tool to help you understand your obligations and prepare your documents. For legally binding advice, always consult a qualified solicitor. For fiqh validation of inheritance calculations, consult a qualified Mufti.
