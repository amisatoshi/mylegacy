# Disclaimer  
**My Legacy — Islamic Estate Planner** is an independent, open-source planning tool developed to help Muslim individuals and families organise their estate planning intentions in accordance with Islamic principles.  
## Legal  
This application does not constitute legal advice. A Will created using My Legacy is a planning aid and must be reviewed and executed under the supervision of a qualified solicitor to be legally valid and enforceable. In England and Wales, a valid Will requires a wet signature by the testator in the presence of two independent witnesses who must also sign — this cannot be replaced by a digital signature alone.
Laws governing Wills, inheritance, and estate administration vary by jurisdiction. Users outside England and Wales should seek local legal advice before relying on any document produced by this application.
## Islamic Fiqh  
The Faraid (Islamic inheritance) calculations in this application are implemented based on classical Sunni fiqh across the Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, and Hanbali madhabs. While every effort has been made to ensure accuracy, this application is not a fatwa and does not replace the guidance of a qualified Islamic scholar or Mufti. Complex inheritance scenarios — including those involving estrangement, disputed paternity, non-Muslim heirs, or assets held in trust — may require scholarly opinion beyond the scope of this tool.  
## Data & Privacy
All data entered into My Legacy is stored locally on your device only, encrypted with AES-256. No data is transmitted to any server, and no account or registration is required. The developers have no access to your data at any time. You are solely responsible for maintaining your password and keeping backups of your data. If your password is lost, your data cannot be recovered.
